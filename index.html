About Us

Holly Integrated Developments Project

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
We are an outstanding youth led, Community-Based Organization, which is non-governmental, non-profit making, non-religious and non-political that operates within Kenya. We were established in 2010 as a Self-help group and later, registered with department of Social Services within the Ministry of Labour and Social Protection as a Community-Based Organization (CBO) with the registration certificate number DSD/RS/CBO/0016/2020.
We strive to improve the lives of vulnerable, and marginalized, youths, women including adolescent girls, and communities in line with the sustainable development goals.
Bridged gap of social inequalities for a more equitable society for all.
