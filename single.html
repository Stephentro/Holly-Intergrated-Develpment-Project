More details about the Organization

We are an outstanding youth led, Community-Based Organization, which is non-governmental, non-profit making, non-religious and non-political that operates within Kenya. We were established in 2010 as a Self-help group and later, registered with department of Social Services within the Ministry of Labour and Social Protection as a Community-Based Organization (CBO) with the registration certificate number DSD/RS/CBO/0016/2020. We aim to improve the lives of vulnerable youths, women, adolescent girls, and communities. The organization focuses on several core areas of service delivery that includes, women and youth empowerment, sustainable development, climate change and environmental initiatives, gender including adolescent girlâ€™s justice, substance abuse prevention, building skills and work opportunities for the youth, advocacy, and campaigns.

Youth Empowerment Program (YEP) aims to empower youth through providing skill-based-training in various fields of development, including but not limited to project management, research, ICT, and entrepreneurship, among others

Assists schools as they address drug and alcohol use among students. Parents are required to participate so that a collaborative approach may be implemented when combating the causes of substance abuse.

Where we get most of our donations

The support received from the County Government of Homabay, along with generous contributions from volunteers and well-wishers, has made a significant impact on our community initiatives. This collaboration has not only provided essential resources but also fostered a spirit of unity and shared purpose. The donations have been instrumental in enhancing local projects, addressing pressing needs, and empowering residents to pursue sustainable development goals.

Moreover, the involvement of volunteers and well-wishers underscores the community's commitment to creating positive change. Their dedication and passion have helped amplify the effects of the donations, ensuring that assistance reaches those who need it most. Together, we are building a stronger, more resilient community, demonstrating that when we come together, we can achieve remarkable things.

How we utilize our donations

We utilize our donations to address critical needs within the community, focusing on essential services such as education, health care, and infrastructure development. A significant portion of the funds is allocated to local schools, providing students with necessary supplies, improving facilities, and supporting extracurricular programs. By investing in education, we aim to empower the next generation and ensure they have the tools to succeed.

In addition to education, donations are also directed toward health initiatives that promote well-being and preventive care. We organize health camps and awareness programs to educate the community about nutrition, sanitation, and disease prevention. By enhancing access to healthcare services, we strive to improve the overall health outcomes of our residents and reduce the burden on local clinics and hospitals.

Lastly, we invest in community development projects that enhance living conditions and promote sustainability. This includes building clean water supply systems, improving sanitation facilities, and supporting local agriculture. By focusing on these areas, we not only improve the quality of life for our residents but also foster long-term growth and resilience within the community. Through careful planning and execution, we ensure that every donation has a meaningful impact.
